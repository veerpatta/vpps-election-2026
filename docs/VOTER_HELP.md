# 🙋 Voter Help

This help page is for students and teachers voting in the VPPS Student Council Election 2026.

## 🔗 Voting Page

https://veerpatta.github.io/vpps-election-2026/#/vote

## 🧭 How to Vote

1. Open the Voting Page.
2. Enter your 6-digit Voting ID.
3. Check the posts shown on the screen.
4. Select one candidate for each post.
5. Review your vote carefully.
6. Submit your final vote.
7. Wait for the success message.

## 🔢 Voting ID

- Each voter gets one 6-digit Voting ID.
- No student password is required.
- Do not share your Voting ID with anyone.
- The same Voting ID cannot be used twice.

## 🏠 House Voting

- Students will see only the house captain posts for their own house.
- Teachers will see all house captain posts.
- Students do not need to choose their house. The app already knows it from the final voter list.

## ⚠️ Important Rules

- Vote only once.
- Choose carefully before final submit.
- After submitting, the vote cannot be changed.
- If your ID does not work, contact the election desk.
- Do not try another student's Voting ID.

## 🆘 If Something Goes Wrong

| Problem | What to Do |
| --- | --- |
| Voting ID not accepted | Check the 6 digits and contact the election desk. |
| Screen says already voted | Contact the election incharge. The same ID cannot vote again. |
| Voting is closed | Wait until the admin opens voting. |
| Internet issue | Inform the election desk and try again when connection is stable. |

## ✅ After Voting

When you see the final success message, your vote has been submitted. You do not need to vote again.
